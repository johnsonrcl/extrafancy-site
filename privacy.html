Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 14, 2026

Introduction

Extra Fancy ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (extrafancy.co) or subscribe to our newsletter.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Newsletter subscription: Email address when you subscribe
  • Contact forms: Name and email when you reach out

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, referring site
  • Device data: Browser type, device type, IP address (anonymized)

How We Use Your Information

  • Send our newsletter and editorial content
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Improve our website and content
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Beehiiv: Newsletter delivery and subscriber management
  • Google Analytics: Website usage tracking (anonymized)
  • LinkedIn: Social sharing and authentication

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who assist our operations (under confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

Your Rights

You may:

  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time via the link in each email
  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@extrafancy.co.

Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed. Upon unsubscribing, your data is removed within 30 days.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and secure third-party services.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
